Cauliflower Tikka Masala

Cauliflower tikka masala or Gobi Tikka Masala is a delectable vegetarian side made with roasted Cauliflower florets cooked in a creamy onion-tomato gravy or sauce. This restaurant favorite curry can be now made easily at home with basic ingredients and step-wise recipe given below.

This sabzi or curry is for cauliflower lovers. It is big on flavors, mildly spicy and makes a perfect weeknight dinner. Can be served with naan, roti OR steamed basmati rice.

Gobi tikka masala can be batch cooked, refrigerated or frozen for meal planning. The curry or sabzi can be customized based on veggies stocked in the refrigerator. The sauce or the gravy can be made ahead in case you are prepping for a large crowd or meal planning.

This is a stove top recipe and roughly takes about 45 minutes from start to finish. Cauliflower tikka masala is vegetarian, gluten free, soy free. It contains nuts and yogurt. Check out the recipe card (notes) for substitutions.

Ingredients

  • Cauliflower
  • Yogurt
  • Coriander powder
  • Roasted cumin powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • Turmeric powder
  • Ginger
  • One big cardamom
  • Two small green cardamom
  • Small piece of cinnamon stick
  • Two green chillies
  • Three cloves
  • One large tomato
  • 12 to 15 cashews

Steps

  1. Cut cauliflower into small pieces.
  2. Add 4 Tbsp of yogurt to a bowl.
  3. Add 1 tsp coriander powder, 1/4 tsp turmeric powder, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp red chili powder, 1/2 tsp garam masala and 1 tsp roasted cumin powder to the bowl and mix well.
  4. Add a drizzle of oil and 1 tbsp besan or chickpea flour and mix.
  5. Add the cauliflower florets and mix well. Leave to marinate.
