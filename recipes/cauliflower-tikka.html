Cauliflower Tikka Masala

Hearty and creamy indian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Cauliflower
  • Yogurt
  • Coriander powder
  • Roasted cumin powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • Turmeric powder
  • Ginger
  • One big cardamom
  • Two small green cardamom
  • Small piece of cinnamon stick
  • Two green chillies
  • Three cloves
  • One large tomato
  • 12 to 15 cashews

Steps

  1. Cut cauliflower into small pieces.
  2. Add 4 Tbsp of yogurt to a bowl.
  3. Add 1 tsp coriander powder, 1/4 tsp turmeric powder, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp red chili powder, 1/2 tsp garam masala and 1 tsp roasted cumin powder to the bowl and mix well.
  4. Add a drizzle of oil and 1 tbsp besan or chickpea flour and mix.
  5. Add the cauliflower florets and mix well. Leave to marinate.
